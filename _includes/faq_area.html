Frequently Asked Questions?

Have some questions about our initiative or how to get involved. Here are some quick responses, but you can also find more comprehensive answers elsewhere in the website or contact us.

What is Reproducibility for Everyone?

A group of volunteers across the globe who are developing resources and teaching workshops to enable researchers to do reproducible research.


Can I contribute content?

Yes, absolutely! Reproducibility for Everyone is a community driven initiative and we welcome anyone to contribute, update, and add to materials. For more information, please see our section on how to contribute.

Where and when is the next workshop taking place?

Upcoming events are listed on our Events page. If you can’t find something suitable there and want to organise your own workshop, let us know?


Can I organise a reproducibility workshop?

Yes, please! All our materials is available under open licenses and we even provide a guide to help you organise your workshop. Complete this form to let us know you’re interested in organising an event so that we can provide some basic support.
